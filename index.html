Dr. APJ Abdual Kalam

"the missle man of India"

15 October 1931 - 27 July 2015

“Don’t take rest after your first victory because if you fail in second, more lips are waiting to say that your first victory was just luck.”

Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007.He played a leading role in devoloping missile and nuclear weapon projects, which rewarded him with a nickname The Missile Man . He was a very simple person who lived an unpretentious lifestyle. He had a keen interest in literature and wrote poems. He never married. He always faced media himself for his failures while let others address for the success. He remained active till the very end. He died while delivering a lecture at the IIM.
